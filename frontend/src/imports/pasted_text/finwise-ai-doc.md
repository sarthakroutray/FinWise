## **1. Introduction**

FinWise AI is an intelligent financial advisory system designed to help users understand their spending behavior, predict future financial trends, and ensure financial safety.

Unlike traditional expense trackers, FinWise AI combines **behavior analysis, predictive modeling, anomaly detection, and AI-driven assistance** to provide personalized, actionable financial guidance.

---

## **2. Problem Statement**

Most individuals struggle with managing their finances effectively due to:

* Lack of awareness about spending habits
* Generic financial advice that doesn’t apply to personal situations
* Inability to predict future financial outcomes
* No systems to detect suspicious or unusual transactions
* Absence of actionable guidance

Existing tools focus on tracking data but fail to provide **intelligent, proactive decision-making support**.

---

## **3. Proposed Solution**

FinWise AI bridges this gap by:

* Analyzing user income and transaction data
* Detecting behavioral spending patterns
* Identifying anomalies and risky transactions
* Predicting future financial trends
* Providing personalized and actionable recommendations
* Offering an AI-powered assistant using contextual data (RAG)
